What is the difference between sole proprietorship, partnership, and corporation ownership?
A sole-proprietorship has one owner who has unlimited liability for the business.

A partnership involves two or more people who combine resources for the business and share profits and losses.

A corporation is considered to be a separate legal entity from its shareholders.
What are the requirements to acquire business permit?
The following are the documents in order to acquire business permit.
  1. Barangay Clearance
  2. Certificate of Registration
  3. Contract of Lease
  4. Certificate of Occupancy
  5. Community Tax Certificate or CEDULA
  6. Fire Safety Inspection Certificate or Fire Permit
  7. Building Permit and Electrical Inspection Certificate
  8. Locational Clearance
  9. Sanitary Permit
